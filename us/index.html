Where It Began

A little history from the opposite perspective

Ana

by Colby

A girl from Brazil reached out to the USA to get new experiences and improve her english. Seeing OKCupid as a good opportunity to make "friends" before arriving, Ana talked to some people and randomly answered a guy who asked her what the first thing she would do in America was. A prompt reply involving "Starbucks" set these two individuals off to a long magical journey of love.

Through the good, the bad, and the late nights playing through the entire Halo, Gears of War collections, and some pretty interesting puzzles, the love that Colby and Ana had for each other grew stronger with every new day and experience. Although stubborn to the idea at first, Colby majestically swept Ana off of her feet, as it were a young girl from Flea Bottom meeting Jon Snow, convincing her to stay in America and live a happy life together forever known as Colbana.
